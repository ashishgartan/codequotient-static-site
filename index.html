What Makes CodeQuotient School of Technology Different?

Learning Methodology

At CodeQuotient School of Technology, we follow a medical college model where students apply their classroom learnings to practical patient care at the hospital. Similarly, our students learn software engineering by developing softwares in CodeQuotient Software Labs.

Companies Engagement

Companies strategize their long-term hiring needs with CodeQuotient School of Technology to build their talent pool. They integrate their developer skills training within the curriculum to help students gain rich software development experience across a range of technologies.

Fees Sponsorships

Our hiring partners sponsor tuition fees for deserving students who are not able to afford formal software engineering education.

Internship

Internships are integrated into our programs to provide students the real-world exposure and they graduate with upto 1.5 years of work experience.
